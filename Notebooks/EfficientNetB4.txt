Developed deep learning models to classify bladded cancer medical images into 4 classes.
Libraries used-
- Tensorflow/Keras: building and training the deep learning model
- NumPy- Numerical operations and data handling
- OpenCV- image preprocessing and resizing
- Matplotlib- visualization of traing accuray and loss graphs
- SCIkit- learn- evaluation metrics and confusion matrix
Training approach- 
-Images were first resized and normalized
-Data augmentations such as rotation and flipping was used. No images processing or color changing augmentation techniques was used.
- A pretrained EfficientNetB4 model was used and fine tuned for bladder cancer  classification.
- The model was trained on near about 65 epochs with batch size of 54 using Adam optimiser
-During training, both traing and validation accuracy and loss was being monitored and at end it was visualised using graphs to ensure no Overfitting and Underfitting.
Preformance- 
Acheived approximately 90% validation accuracy
